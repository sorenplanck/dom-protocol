Problem:
The audit identified a critical consensus gap: full block validation does not sufficiently enforce aggregate economic balance across inputs, outputs, fees, reward, and kernels.

Objective:
Make aggregate block-balance validation mandatory for every accepted block.

Tasks:
1. Locate the canonical block validation function(s) and all acceptance paths.
2. Implement or wire aggregate balance validation at block level.
3. Validate the equation across the whole block, not merely per transaction.
4. Reject blocks that:

   - live block relay;
   - IBD;
   - chain extension;
   - reorg promotion;
