TASK 28/50 — IMPLEMENT DETERMINISTIC MEMPOOL REINJECTION AFTER REORG

Problem:
Transactions from disconnected blocks may become valid again after reorg. Without deterministic reinjection, nodes can diverge in mempool state.

Objective:
Reinject eligible transactions from disconnected blocks into mempool in canonical order.

Tasks:
1. During reorg, collect txs from disconnected blocks.
2. Exclude coinbase and invalid/system transactions.
3. Revalidate each tx against the new canonical UTXO.
4. Resolve conflicts deterministically.
5. Insert in canonical order.
6. Add tests:
   - tx from disconnected block returns to mempool;
   - invalid tx after reorg is not reinjected;
   - conflicting txs produce deterministic outcome;
   - nodes with different delivery order converge to same mempool digest.
